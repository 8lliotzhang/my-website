15 Jan 2026 - on the proposed reactivation of Ottawa Union Station

We are building a New Canada, and we will bring rail back to Ottawa Union Station. It is a symbol of our national rejuvenation, of our rebirth, and of connection to our roots. Yes, we are a modern nation, with a diverse progressive and healthy democracy and a strong diversified economy, one quite different to the Confederation of 1871. But that shared national myth remains important to our heritage and identity when we call ourselves Canadians.

Ottawa, the city linking Toronto and Montreal, Ontario and Quebec, Upper and Lower Canada, will once again have a rail connection hub for all peoples in the very heart of downtown. Furthermore, such placement would be extremely convenient, within walking distance of Byward and Parliament, especially with a walking tunnel connection to the nearby Rideau LRT Line 1 station. In every category, (except for maybe environmental, I'm not sure, I haven't done the studies) the reactivation of Ottawa Union Station is clearly the best choice.
