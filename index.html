Verifiery

A project with Charles Zhu! We used ChatGPT output and combined it with word frequency analysis to detect AI-generated text to reduce false accusations of students! We won 3rd Place at the ORSF and the Digital Technology award yay.

adsb-near-me

This cool little web app finds where you are and then tells you where the nearest plane is! Built with python, integrated with HTML via pyscript, hosted by onrender. A small, functional, complete summer project. A fun distraction to work on.

Try it out (expect up to 1min delay when opening)
